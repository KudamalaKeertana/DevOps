Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish consisting of layers of wide pasta noodles, rich tomato sauce, seasoned ground beef or Italian sausage, creamy ricotta cheese, and gooey melted mozzarella, all baked to perfection. It's hearty, comforting, and perfect for feeding a crowd.

  • Ground Beef or Italian Sausage
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Ricotta Cheese

Ravioli

Ravioli are small squares of pasta dough filled with a variety of savory ingredients such as cheese, meat, or vegetables. These little pockets of deliciousness are typically served with a flavorful sauce, like marinara or sage butter, and topped with grated Parmesan cheese for a satisfying meal.

  • Pasta Dough
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese

Tiramisu

Tiramisu is a decadent Italian dessert made with layers of espresso-soaked ladyfingers, creamy mascarpone cheese, and a dusting of cocoa powder. Its name translates to "pick me up," and with its rich coffee flavor and indulgent texture, it's sure to do just that.

  • Ladyfingers (Savoiardi Biscuits)
  • Mascarpone Cheese
  • Espresso Coffee

Chili

Chili is a hearty stew made with ground beef, beans, tomatoes, and chili peppers, along with a blend of spices like chili powder, cumin, and paprika. It's a comfort food favorite, perfect for chilly days and gatherings with friends and family, and can be customized with toppings like cheese, sour cream, and diced onions.

  • Ground Beef
  • Kidney Beans
  • Diced Tomatoes
